Measurement Tips

  • Chest/Bust: Measure around the fullest part of your chest/bust, keeping the tape measure parallel to the floor.
  • Waist: Measure around the narrowest part of your waist, usually just above the belly button.
  • Hips: Measure around the fullest part of your hips, keeping the tape measure parallel to the floor.
  • Inseam: Measure from the top of your inner thigh to the bottom of your ankle.
